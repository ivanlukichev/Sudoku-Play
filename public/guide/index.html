Sudoku Guide

The Guide section is the educational home of Sudoku-Play.org. It collects clear articles about how to play Sudoku, the rules behind the grid, solving tips, beginner advice, and child-friendly puzzle formats. If you want to move between the classic game and the kids area without hunting for help, this is where everything is organized in one place.

You can use these guides in two ways. Read them as a step-by-step learning path if you are new to Sudoku, or dip in when you want a quick explanation of a specific topic such as strategy, Mini Sudoku, or Picture Sudoku. When you are ready to practise, jump straight into the Sudoku generator, the main Sudoku game, or the Kids Sudoku section.

How to Use This Guide

If you are just starting, begin with How to Play Sudoku, then move to Sudoku Rules Explained and Sudoku for Beginners. If you already know the basics and want to improve, go next to Sudoku Tips and Sudoku Strategies.

For families, teachers, and parents, the kids articles connect the educational side of the site with the actual play area. You can read Sudoku for Kids, then move directly into Mini Sudoku or Picture Sudoku without leaving the main Guide flow.

If your goal is a calmer and more readable playing experience, move into the accessibility cluster to learn when to use Row and Column Highlight, Focus Mode, Dyslexia Mode, and Night Mode.
