What the Extension Does

Sudoku Play for browsers is a lightweight launcher. Instead of searching for the site or leaving a pinned tab open all day, you can click the toolbar icon and jump straight to the puzzle you want.

Play Sudoku

Open the main 9x9 board and switch between Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert.

Daily Sudoku

Reach the shared daily challenge instantly when you want a consistent routine puzzle.

Kids Sudoku

Launch Mini Sudoku, Picture Sudoku, and Junior Sudoku from the same popup.

Guide

Keep the Sudoku Guide close by for rules, tips, strategies, and beginner help.
