How to Play Mini Sudoku

Each row, each column, and each 2x2 box must contain the numbers 1, 2, 3, and 4 exactly once. Because the grid is small, children can focus on the core rule without too many empty spaces at once.

Why Mini Sudoku Helps

Mini Sudoku teaches logic, observation, and patient checking. It works well as a first puzzle because every move stays visible and easy to understand.
