Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains the basic approach to information handling on Sudoku-Play.org. The site is designed as a simple browser-based Sudoku experience without account creation, profile storage, or public user content.

Information We Collect

Sudoku-Play.org does not ask you to create an account or submit personal profile details to play Sudoku. Like most websites, the server hosting the site may process standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, and timestamps in access logs.

How Information Is Used

Basic technical information may be used to keep the site running, monitor uptime, review errors, understand broad usage patterns, and protect the service from abuse. The goal is to maintain site performance and reliability, not to build user profiles.

Cookies and Local Storage

This MVP does not require login cookies to function. Browser storage may be used in the future for lightweight game preferences or progress convenience features, but the first version focuses on direct play with minimal persistence.

Third-Party Services

If analytics, hosting infrastructure, or other third-party services are added later, those providers may process standard technical data according to their own policies. Sudoku-Play.org aims to keep the stack lightweight and avoid unnecessary data collection.

Children's Privacy

Sudoku-Play.org is a general audience puzzle site. It is not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as the site grows. Material changes should be reflected on this page together with a revised effective date.
