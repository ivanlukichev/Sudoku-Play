How to Play Junior Sudoku

Each row and column must contain the numbers 1 to 6 exactly once. Each 2x3 box must also contain the numbers 1 to 6 exactly once. The puzzle is larger than Mini Sudoku, but still much easier to read than a full 9x9 board.

Why Junior Sudoku Matters

Junior Sudoku is the bridge between starter puzzles and classic Sudoku. It gives children more space to think while keeping the rules manageable.
