# Opera Add-ons Draft

## Short Description
Play 10 quick 4x4 popup levels in Opera, then continue to the full Sudoku board.

## Promo Text
Quick popup Sudoku one click away.

## Full Description
Sudoku Play for Opera gives you a fast popup Sudoku experience right from the browser toolbar.

Open the popup and choose what you want to play:

- Play 10 mini popup levels
- Open the full 9x9 board
- Open Daily Sudoku on the website
- Open Kids Sudoku on the website
- Open the Guide

The extension is designed as a lightweight companion to https://sudoku-play.org/ so you can solve a quick puzzle in the popup, then move to the larger board or the website for more modes.

Highlights:

- compact popup UI
- 10 popup mini Sudoku levels
- completion modal after each solved level
- built-in 9x9 Sudoku gameplay
- keyboard support and fast input
- quick launch for the daily puzzle
- easy access to kids-friendly Sudoku modes
- link back to the full guide and website
