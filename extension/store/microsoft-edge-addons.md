# Microsoft Edge Add-ons Draft

## Short Description
Play 10 quick 4x4 popup levels in Edge, then continue to the full Sudoku board.

## Promo Text
Quick Sudoku from your Edge toolbar.

## Full Description
Sudoku Play for Microsoft Edge gives you a fast Sudoku break directly from the browser toolbar.

Open the popup and choose what you want to do:

- play 10 built-in 4x4 mini levels
- continue to the next level after solving one
- open the full 9x9 board inside the extension
- open Daily Sudoku on the website
- open Kids Sudoku on the website
- open the Sudoku Guide

The extension is designed as a lightweight companion to https://sudoku-play.org/. It keeps quick puzzles close while still giving you one-click access to the full website when you want more modes, larger boards, or learning material.

Highlights:

- compact popup UI
- 10 popup mini Sudoku levels
- completion modal after each solved level
- built-in 9x9 Sudoku gameplay
- keyboard support and fast input
- quick launch for the daily puzzle
- easy access to kids-friendly Sudoku modes
- link back to the full guide and website
