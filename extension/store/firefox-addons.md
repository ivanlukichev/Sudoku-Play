# Firefox Add-ons Draft

## Short Description
Play 10 quick 4x4 popup levels in Firefox, then continue to the full Sudoku board.

## Promo Text
Quick Sudoku from your toolbar.

## Full Description
Sudoku Play is a small browser extension that lets you solve fast Sudoku mini levels in the popup and continue into a full 9x9 board when you want more.

From the popup you can:

- play 10 built-in 4x4 mini levels
- continue to the next level after each solved grid
- open the full 9x9 Sudoku board inside the extension
- open the daily shared puzzle on the website
- jump to Kids Sudoku on the website
- visit the Sudoku Guide

The extension keeps the interface simple, clean, and focused. It is built for players who want a quick brain break in the popup, with an easy path to a larger Sudoku session afterward.

Version 1 focuses on:

- 10 popup mini levels
- completion modal with Next Level and Open Full Board
- a playable 9x9 Sudoku board inside the extension
- keyboard support and fast input
- a branded popup launcher
- Daily Sudoku shortcut
- Kids Sudoku shortcut
- lightweight Firefox-compatible Manifest V3 setup
