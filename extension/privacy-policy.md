# Sudoku Play Extension Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Sudoku Play browser extension builds in this folder, including Chrome, Edge, Opera, and Firefox versions.

## What the extension does

Sudoku Play includes:

- a 4x4 popup mini-game with multiple levels
- a built-in 9x9 Sudoku board inside the extension
- buttons that can open Sudoku Play website pages such as Daily Sudoku, Kids Sudoku, and the Guide

## Data the extension does not collect

The extension is designed to work locally and does not intentionally collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

- name
- email address
- phone number
- payment information
- browsing history
- web page content
- keystrokes outside the extension interface
- contacts
- location data

## Data storage inside the extension

The current extension version does not require account creation and does not use remote user databases.

Gameplay in the popup and built-in board is intended to run locally inside the extension package. The extension does not use cookies.

## Network activity

The extension itself is designed to avoid background tracking and remote analytics inside the extension UI.

Network activity may happen only when you intentionally choose to open pages on [sudoku-play.org](https://sudoku-play.org/), such as:

- Daily Sudoku
- Kids Sudoku
- Guide pages
- the main website

When you open the website from the extension, the website is governed by the site privacy policy:

[https://sudoku-play.org/privacy-policy/](https://sudoku-play.org/privacy-policy/)

## Permissions

The extension uses only the minimum behavior needed to open its own extension pages and to open selected Sudoku Play website links in a new browser tab.

The extension is not designed to read browsing history, inspect page contents, or monitor activity on unrelated websites.

## Children

Sudoku Play includes kids-friendly puzzle modes, but the extension is not designed to collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the extension changes. If the extension later adds storage, analytics, sync, accounts, or new permissions, this document should be updated to reflect that behavior.
